# Football Headquarters — Marketing Copy

## App Store Description

Build your football program from the ground up — grow your stadium, train your roster, and level up star heroes with Game Balls. Then load the bus, storm rival stadiums in real-time raids, and run your plays until you've silenced the crowd and taken their house. Football Headquarters is loud, hype, and 100% bloodless — play free at football-headquarters.vercel.app.

## Feature Bullets

- **Build your program** — grow your Stadium, Training Field, and Scouting Dept from a sandlot facility into a powerhouse.
- **Recruit and level a real roster** — scout new talent, run drills, and evolve your heroes into 5-star Game Ball legends with Scout Search.
- **Storm their house** — real-time raids: send in your squad, call the Blitz, bring the Mascot and Fan Mob, and leave their stands empty.
- **Climb the season ladder** — 12 games from the Preseason Opener all the way to THE CHAMPIONSHIP, with Game Ball ratings on every stage.
- **Defend home field** — build up your Defense Rating and make rivals pay when they try to take your house.
- **Go async with Live Rivals** — publish your stadium and raid real players' programs; check your Defense Log to see who came for your crowd.
- **Chase the trophy ladder** — grind your way from Sandlot all the way to G.O.A.T. status, one silenced crowd at a time, fueled by Daily Practice quests for Crowns.
