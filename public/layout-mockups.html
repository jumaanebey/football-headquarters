🎯 Bullseye Layouts

Rule for all three: field in the middle → your stuff in rings moving out → the outermost ring is open ground where attackers land. Walls never touch the island edge — raiders need somewhere to stand. Diagrams are top-down (12×12 shown: the outer 1-tile border is the landing apron beyond the 10×10 board); the tilted view approximates the in-game camera.
