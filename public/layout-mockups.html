🎯 Pick-Your-Formation Layouts

All three ship in the game — players choose their formation in the Front Office (fixed spots within each, so zero placement bugs; switching formations is free, your upgrade levels carry over). Every formation follows the bullseye rule: the Field in the middle → your club in rings moving out → the outer ring is open ground where attackers land.

The pieces (every tile, named)

  • 🏟 The Field — your Stadium. They take it, you lose.
  • WR — War Room · game-planning facility
  • SC — Scouting Dept · recruiting facility
  • RH — Rehab Center · energy facility
  • TF — Training Field · drills facility
  • J — JUGS Machine · rapid-fire football launcher
  • SL — Tackling Sled · short range, hits like a truck
  • RT — Ref Tower · penalty flags SLOW runners, longest range
  • TC — T-Shirt Cannon · splash, blasts the whole cluster
  • ⭐ Hero Post — your 2 strongest heroes patrol here on defense
  • 🚌 Team Bus — parks a gate shut (big blocker)
  • ▦ Blocking-Sled Walls + dashed = GATES (funnels)
  • ▩ Attack Zone — raiders land on the dark outer ring
